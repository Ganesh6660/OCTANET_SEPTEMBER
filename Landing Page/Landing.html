01 MADONE SLR STORY

The Madone SLR 7 Gen 8 is a high-performance bike that combines the best of both worlds- the aerodynamics of the previous Madone and the lightweight design of the Emonda. Trek's goal was to create a bike that is fast, comfortable, and versatile, and the Madone SLR 7 Gen 8 seems to deliver on all fronts.

One of the standout features of this bike is its ability to balance aerodynamics and weight, making it an excellent choice for riders who want a bike that can handle both sprints and long climbs. The Madone SLR 7 Gen 8 also features a new tube shape that improves aerodynamics and reduces weight, making it a formidable competitor in the world of high-performance bikes.

In terms of specs, the Madone SLR 7 Gen 8 comes with a 900 Series OCLV Carbon frame, Full System Foil tube shaping, and a threaded T47 bottom bracket. It also features a Shimano Ultegra R8170 Di2 shifter, Shimano Ultegra R8150 Di2 front derailleur, and Shimano Ultegra R8150 Di2 rear derailleur.
